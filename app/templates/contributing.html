How you can contribute

Running a website requires a lot of work, and a larger team of people tends to ease the load off of that work.

This is where you come in. Do you think that the codebase (found here) for this website needs improving? Make an issue or pull request, or just contact me! Do you think that the design looks unnecessarily like a Picasso? Tell me and provide suggestions!

If you have any suggestions to improving this website or want to help out please do not hesitate to contact me.
