What is Nuncio?

Nuncio is the simple-to-use news site dedicated to curating any and all news.

Nuncio is also a site that strives for quality in both it's user interactions and in the links shown.

More unimportantly, the word nuncio stands for the papal ambassador, however this is not how the name came up.

Why Make Nuncio in the first place?

Nuncio is the response to some of the problems presented in modern news aggregators , some problems being unique to each platform and some being a problem found in all platforms.

These problems include the personalisation of the news a user can see, a "personal front page", a lack of a strong voting system to allow users to actually curate their news, and the entrenchment of people's ideas through the unintentional creation of echo chambers.

Nuncio manages to solve all of these problems by doing the following things:

  • Have the current highest voted links appear as the main page, no personalisation done by the user so therefore no cherrypicking.
  • Use a simple yet strong voting system to order posts by what users consider to be an important and good quality news article.
  • Use a tagging system for better organisation and searching of articles you are interested in, for example to see news about your home city or a specific topic for research.
